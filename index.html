Dedicated to the Welfare of Those Who Serve Uganda

Wazalendo SACCO was founded to provide affordable financial services to Uganda People's Defence Force personnel and civilian employees of the Ministry of Defence. We believe every person who serves this nation deserves a secure financial future.

From humble beginnings at Bombo Barracks, we have grown into one of Uganda's largest SACCOs — operating branches across every military installation and managing over UGX 2 trillion in member savings.

Savings Products

Choose a savings plan that fits your service life — from flexible daily deposits to structured long-term goals.

Regular Savings

Monthly deductions from your salary. Simple, automatic, and stress-free. Earns 4% p.a. interest.

  • Minimum UGX 50,000 / month
  • Withdraw anytime
  • Automatic salary deduction

Fixed Deposit

Lock in your savings for 6 – 24 months and earn higher guaranteed returns of up to 12% p.a.

  • Minimum UGX 500,000
  • Tenors: 6, 12, 24 months
  • Certificate issued on maturity

Education Fund

Dedicated savings account for your children's school fees. Earns 6% p.a. — withdrawable only per school term.

  • Open in child's name
  • Minimum UGX 20,000 / month
  • Scholarships for top pupils

Our Services

Beyond savings and loans — a complete financial ecosystem built for defence personnel.

Mobile Banking

Check balances, request loans, and transfer funds — all on your phone via MTN Mobile Money or Airtel Money.

Real Estate Investment

Participate in WSACCO real estate projects — commercial complexes in Nakasongola, Arua, and Kampala.

Insurance Products
